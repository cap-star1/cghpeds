Why choose Hostplus?

There are a whole lot of positives when it comes to partnering with Hostplus as an adviser. Why? To start with, your clients could benefit by investing in a top-performing fund1 over both the short and long term.

And while our strong performance may support your clients’ objectives, we know it’s just as important that we make it easy for you to work with us. That’s why we’ve established a dedicated adviser support team. They’ll work with you to develop tools and processes to improve efficiencies that not only benefit you, but also your clients. A super fund that helps both you and your clients reach your goals? That's a plus.
